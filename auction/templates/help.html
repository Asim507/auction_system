Need Assistance?

Find answers to your questions and get support for your online auctions.

How do I bid on an auction? ▼

To place a bid, navigate to the auction listing and enter your bid amount before the auction ends.

How do I list an item for auction? ▼

Go to "My Account" and select "Sell an Item." Fill in the required details and set your auction duration.

What happens if I win an auction? ▼

If you win, you will be notified via email and can proceed to complete your payment.
